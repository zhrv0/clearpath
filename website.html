Most apps tell you to "just go outside" or "think positive."

We know that's not how it works.

Some days the bravest thing you do is make your bed. Some days you don't leave the house at all. That's okay. That still counts.

the process

Simple enough for
your hardest days

01
Check in daily

Mark another sober day. Watch your streak grow. Every day you check in, you earn points — and your streak bonus compounds the longer you go.

02
Choose your quests

Pick from tiny at-home tasks, gentle outdoor steps, or social challenges. Start with drinking a glass of water. There's no wrong place to begin.

03
Use the calm tools

Box breathing, grounding exercises, body scans. When anxiety spikes, open the app. The tools are short, simple, and actually work.

04
Write it down

Your private journal is yours alone. Vent, ramble, cry on the page. No one reads it. It's just a safe place to put the things you can't say out loud.

what's inside

Built for the whole journey

Core

Sobriety Streak & Points

Check in every day to build your streak. The longer you go, the bigger the bonus. Watch your points grow into rank milestones — from Seedling all the way to Phoenix.

Quests

Daily Quests for Every Day

Three categories — at home, going outside, and social. Whether you're in bed or out in the world, there's always something small you can do.

Anxiety

Calm Garden Tools

Box breathing, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, body scans, mindful walks. Six tools to interrupt anxiety before it takes over.

Private

Your Notebook

A completely private journal. Write entries, give them titles, go back and read them. No one else can see it. Ever.

Anchor

Why I Stay

Add notes and photos of your reasons. For the moments when you forget why you started. Open it and remember.
